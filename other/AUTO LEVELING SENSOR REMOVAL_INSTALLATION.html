AUTO LEVELING SENSOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id091800805700

1.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2.  Jack up the vehicle and remove the wheel and tire. (LR)

3.  Disconnect the connector.

am3zzw00001821

4.  Remove the bolts, then remove the auto leveling sensor.

5.  Install in the reverse order of removal.

6.  Perform the auto leveling system initialization. (See AUTO LEVELING SYSTEM INITIALIZATION.)
