ACCELERATOR PEDAL POSITION (APP) SENSOR INSPECTION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)]

id0140b2803200


Visual Inspection

1.  Remove the APP. (See ACCELERATOR PEDAL (APP) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

2.  Verify that there are no metal shavings on the sensor.

3.  Visually inspect the APP sensor for the following:

•  Damage
•  Cracks
•  Rusted sensor terminal
•  Bent sensor terminal
―  If there is any malfunction, replace the APP sensor.

4.  Install the APP. (See ACCELERATOR PEDAL (APP) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)


Voltage Inspection

1.  Connect the oscilloscope to the accelerator pedal connector terminal E and body ground.

2.  Turn the engine switch to the ON position (Engine off).

3.  Verify that the duty ratio at terminal E changes while operating the accelerator pedal as shown in the graph.

Resistance Inspection

1.  Disconnect the APP sensor connector.

2.  Measure the resistance between terminals A and C.

•  If it is not as specified, replace the APP sensor.

Circuit Open/Short Inspection

1.  Disconnect the PCM connector. (See PCM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

2.  Inspect the following wiring harness for an open or short circuit (continuity check).

am3zzw00001583


Open circuit

•  If there is no continuity, there is an open circuit. Repair or replace the wiring harness.
―  APP sensor terminal A and instrument cluster terminal 2X
―  APP sensor terminal B and instrument cluster terminal 2T
―  APP sensor terminal C and instrument cluster terminal 2R
―  APP sensor terminal D and ground
―  APP sensor terminal E and PCM terminal 310
―  APP sensor terminal F and PJB terminal Q


Short circuit

•  If there is continuity, there is a short circuit. Repair or replace the wiring harness.
―  APP sensor terminal A and power supply
―  APP sensor terminal A and body ground
―  APP sensor terminal B and power supply
―  APP sensor terminal B and body ground
―  APP sensor terminal C and power supply
―  APP sensor terminal C and body ground
―  APP sensor terminal D and power supply
―  APP sensor terminal D and body ground
―  APP sensor terminal E and power supply
―  APP sensor terminal F and body ground
