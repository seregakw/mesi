SIDE STEP MOLDING REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1.  Remove the fasteners.

2.  While cutting the double-sided adhesive tape using a flathead screwdriver or a razor, separate the side step molding from the body.

Warning
•  Using a razor with bare hands can cause injury. Always wear gloves when using a razor.

3.  Pull the side step molding outward, then disengage clips A from the body.

4.  Slide the side step molding towards the front of the vehicle and remove clips B from the side step molding.

am3zzw00002311

5.  Remove the side step molding.

6.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
