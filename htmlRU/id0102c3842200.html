DTC B1317

Fuel additive control module B+ voltage high
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The fuel additive control module monitors the supply voltage of battery positive terminal at fuel additive control module terminals 1 and 8. If the Fuel additive control module detected battery positive terminal voltage above 16 V, the fuel additive control module determines that the power supply system has malfunction.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Short to power circuit between generator connector terminal D and PCM connector terminal 107
•  Generator malfunction
•  Fuel additive control module and/or generator are poorly connected
