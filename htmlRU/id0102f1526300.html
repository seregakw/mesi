DTC P2105

Intake shutter valve actuator control system - forced engine shutdown
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the engine speed for specified period after the engine shutdown. If the PCM detects that the engine speed is not dropped for specified period after the engine switch is off. The PCM determines that the engine is not shutdown.
Diagnostic support note
•  The FREEZE FRAME DATA is not available.
•  The DTC is stored in the PCM memory.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Engine oil deterioration (Appears an excessive oil mist in blow by gas as the engine continues run)
•  Fuel injector malfunction
•  Engine internal components malfunction (Burning/seizure, abnormal combustion)
•  PCM malfunction
