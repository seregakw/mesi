TURBOCHARGER INSPECTION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)]

id0113b4801000

1.  Remove the air hose. (See INTAKE-AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

2.  Visually inspect the turbocharger rotor vanes for damage.

•  If there is visible damage to the rotor vanes, clean the intake-air system then replace the turbocharger.
