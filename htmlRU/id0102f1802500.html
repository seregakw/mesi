DTC P0089

Fuel pressure regulator performance
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the fuel pressure in the common rail from the fuel pressure sensor while the engine running. If the fuel pressure drops before it reaches the fuel pressure limiter regulating pressure while supply pump and suction pump fail-safe function, the PCM determines that the fuel pressure limiter regulating performance problem.
Diagnostic support note
•  The MIL does not illuminate.
•  The FREEZE FRAME DATA is not available.
•  The DTC is stored in the PCM memory.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Fuel pressure limiter malfunction
•  Fuel leakage between supply pump and fuel injector through common rail
•  PCM malfunction
