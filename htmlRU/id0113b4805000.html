VARIABLE BOOST CONTROL (VBC) VALVE ACTUATOR INSPECTION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)]

id0113b4805000

1.  Remove the air hose. (See INTAKE-AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

2.  Disconnect the vacuum hose from the variable boost control (VBC) valve actuator.

am3zzw00001457

3.  Connect a vacuum pump to the variable boost control (VBC) valve actuator.

4.  Operate the hand vacuum pump and observe the reading.

•  If the vacuum pump does not hold vacuum, replace the turbocharger .
am3zzw00001458

5.  Apply vacuum of 65 kPa {0.66 kgf/cm2, 9.4 pis} and measure the distance travelled by the vacuum diaphragm unit actuator rod.

•  If the rod does not move between 9—11 mm {0.35—0.43 in}, replace the turbocharger .
