DTC P0504

Brake light switch and brake signal correlation
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors changes in input signal for brake light switch and brake signal (from instrument cluster). If the PCM does not detect brake light switch signal for 1 sec. after the brake signal inputted from the instrument cluster, the PCM determines that the brake light switch circuit has malfunction.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Brake light switch malfunction
•  Open or short circuit between brake light switch and PCM terminal 317
•  Open or short circuit between brake light switch and ground through brake light
•  PCM malfunction
