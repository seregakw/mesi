DTC P2409

Fuel-filler switch circuit range/performance
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The fuel additive control module monitors the fuel-filler lid open/close position using fuel-filler switch. If the fuel additive control module detects that the fuel-filler lid open while the vehicle is driven or fuel-filler lid closed while refueling 10 liter {2.64 US gal, 2.20 Imp gal} or more, the fuel additive control module determines that the fuel-filler switch circuit range/performance problem.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Open circuit between fuel additive control module terminal 16 and fuel-filler switch terminal A
•  Open circuit between fuel additive control module terminal 15 and fuel-filler switch terminal B
•  Short circuit between fuel additive control module terminal 15 and 16
•  Fuel-filler lid stuck open or close
•  Fuel-filler switch malfunction
•  Short to ground circuit between fuel additive control module terminal 15 and fuel-filler switch terminal B
