DTC P0088

Fuel rail pressure - too high
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the fuel pressure in the common rail from the fuel pressure sensor while the engine is running. If the fuel pressure is more than threshold, the PCM determines that the fuel pressure is too high.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Fuel metering valve malfunction (built-in fuel pump)
•  Fuel pump malfunction
•  Fuel pressure sensor malfunction
•  Fuel injector malfunction
•  Fuel injector improper connection
•  Leakage or clogging in fuel line
•  Fuel pipe improper connection on the fuel tank
•  PCM malfunction
