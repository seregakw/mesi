DTC P0112

Intake air temperature (IAT) sensor circuit low input
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the IAT sensor signal. If the voltage at PCM terminal 231 remains low, the PCM determines that there is an IAT sensor circuit problem.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  IAT sensor malfunction
•  Connector or terminal malfunction
•  Short to ground in the wiring harness between IAT sensor terminal F and PCM terminal 231
•  PCM malfunction
