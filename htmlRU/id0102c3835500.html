DTC P1201

Fuel injector No.1 circuit malfunction
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the fuel injector No.1. If the voltage at PCM terminals 118 and 101 remains high, the PCM determines that there is a fuel injector No.1 circuit problem.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Fuel injector No.1 malfunction
•  Connector or terminal malfunction
•  Short to power supply in the wiring harness between fuel injector No.1 terminal A and PCM terminal 118
•  Short to power supply in the wiring harness between fuel injector No.1 terminal B and PCM terminal 101
•  PCM malfunction
