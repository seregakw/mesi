Осторожно
•  Water penetrating the connector will cause sensor malfunction. To prevent this, be careful not to damage the wiring harnesses or the waterproof connector so as to cause water penetration.
Примечание
•  Before performing the following inspection, make sure to follow the procedure as indicated in the troubleshooting flowchart. (See ПОРЯДОК ИСПОЛЬЗОВАНИЯ ДАННОГО РУКОВОДСТВА.)

Voltage Inspection

1.  Start the engine and warm up the engine completely.

2.  Measure the voltage between fuel pressure sensor terminal C and body ground using a voltmeter at idle.

CIrcuit Open/Short Inspection

1.  Disconnect the PCM connector. (See PCM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

2.  Inspect the following wiring harness for open or short circuit (continuity check).

am3zzw00001586


Open circuit

•  If there is no continuity, there is an open circuit. Repair or replace the wiring harness.
―  Fuel pressure sensor terminal C and PCM terminal 219
―  Fuel pressure sensor terminal B and PCM terminal 203
―  Fuel pressure sensor terminal A and PCM terminal 202


Short circuit

•  If there is continuity, there is an short circuit. Repair or replace the wiring harness.
―  Fuel pressure sensor terminal A and power supply
―  Fuel pressure sensor terminal A and body ground
―  Fuel pressure sensor terminal B and power supply
―  Fuel pressure sensor terminal B and body ground
―  Fuel pressure sensor terminal C and power supply
―  Fuel pressure sensor terminal C and body ground
