SUPPLY PUMP INSPECTION [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)]

id0114f1805800

Caution
•  Special tools and testers are required when disassembling the supply pump. Disassembling the supply pump without special tools and testers will cause a malfunction.
•  Consult an authorized DENSO parts distributor for repair if any of the following supply pump parts are possibly malfunctioning.
―  Supply pump inner parts
―  Fuel temperature sensor

1.  Verify that the gear is in neutral position.

2.  Start the engine to verify the engine operation condition.

•  If any one of the following conditions occurs, replace the suction control valve. (See SUCTION CONTROL VALVE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)].)
―  Engine stall
―  Rough idling
―  Lack of power
•  If any one of the following conditions occurs, replace the supply pump. (See SUPPLY PUMP REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)].)
―  Engine cannot be started
―  Rough engine starting
