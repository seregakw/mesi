DTC P0203

Fuel injector No.3 control circuit open
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the input voltage from the fuel injector No.3. If the voltages at PCM deviates from the threshold, the PCM determines that the there is a fuel injector No.3 circuit problem.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Fuel injector No.3 malfunction
•  Connector or terminal malfunction
•  Open circuit in the wiring harness between fuel injector No.3 terminal B and PCM terminal 109
•  Open circuit in the wiring harness between fuel injector No.3 terminal A and PCM terminal 125
•  PCM malfunction
