REAR BELTLINE MOLDING REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1.  Remove the sail garnish. (See SAIL GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2.  Remove the rear door garnish. (See REAR DOOR GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3.  Raise the center of the rear beltline molding and detach tabs A.

4.   Using a small flathead screwdriver, detach clip B and remove the rear beltline molding.

5.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
