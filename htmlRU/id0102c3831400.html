DTC P0380

Glow plug circuit malfunction
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the input voltage from the glow plug relay signal. If the voltages at PCM deviates from the threshold, the PCM determines that the there is a glow plug relay system problem.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Glow plug relay malfunction
•  Glow plug malfunction
•  Connector or terminal malfunction
•  Open circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal A and fuse
•  Open circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal D and PCM terminal 132
•  Open circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal C and PCM terminal 210
•  Short to ground circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal D and PCM terminal 132
•  Short to ground circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal C and PCM terminal 210
•  Short to power supply in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal D and PCM terminal 132
•  Short to power supply in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal C and PCM terminal 210
•  Open circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal D and glow plug terminal A
•  Open circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal D and glow plug terminal B
•  Open circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal D and glow plug terminal C
•  Open circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal D and glow plug terminal D
•  Short to ground circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal D and glow plug terminal A
•  Short to ground circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal D and glow plug terminal B
•  Short to ground circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal D and glow plug terminal C
•  Short to ground circuit in wiring harness between glow plug relay terminal D and glow plug terminal D
•  PCM malfunction
