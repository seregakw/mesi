MIL ILLUMINATES
DESCRIPTION
•  The MIL illuminates incorrectly.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  The PCM illuminates due to emission-related concern (DTC is stored in PCM)
•  Instrument cluster malfunction
Примечание
•  If the MIL blinks at a steady rate, a misfire condition could possibly exist.

Diagnostic procedure

STEP

INSPECTION

RESULTS

ACTION
1
Connect the M-MDS to the DLC-2.
Turn the engine switch to the ON position.
Retrieve any DTC.
Are DTCs displayed?
Yes
DTC is displayed:
•  Go to appropriate DTC test.
No
No DTC is displayed:
•  Inspect instrument cluster operation.
2
Verify test results.
•  If normal, return to the diagnostic index to service any additional symptoms. (See SYMPTOM DIAGNOSTIC INDEX [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)
•  If the malfunction remains, inspect the related Service Information and perform repair or diagnosis.
―  If the vehicle is repaired, troubleshooting is completed.
―  If the vehicle is not repaired or additional diagnostic information is not available, replace the PCM. (See PCM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)
