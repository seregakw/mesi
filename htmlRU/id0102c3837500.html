DTC P1412

EGR valve frozen
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the input voltage from the EGR valve position sensor signal. If the voltages at PCM terminal 129 deviates from the threshold, the PCM determines that the there is a EGR valve malfunction.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  EGR valve servo motor malfunction
•  Contaminate on EGR valve
•  EGR valve position sensor malfunction
•  Vent or damage in the wiring harness between EGR valve servo motor and PCM
•  PCM malfunction
