ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE (ECT) SENSOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)]

id0140b2801900

1.  Remove the battery cover. (See СНЯТИЕ И УСТАНОВКА АККУМУЛЯТОРНОЙ БАТАРЕИ [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove the engine cover.

4.  Drain the engine coolant. (See ЗАМЕНА ОХЛАЖДАЮЩЕЙ ЖИДКОСТИ [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

5.  Disconnect ECT sensor connector

6.  Remove in the order indicated in the table.

am3zzw00001564

1
Clip
2
O-ring
3
ECT sensor

7.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
