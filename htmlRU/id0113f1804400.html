GLOW PLUG REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)]

id0113f1804400

Caution
•  Do not damage the heated section of the glow plug.
•  Do not reuse a glow plug that has been dropped over a height of 10 cm {0.4 in}, even when there are no scratches and resistance is normal (approx. 0.6 ohms [20°C {68°F}] (Reference value)).
•  Do not remove the glow plug unless you are replacing it.

1.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2.  Remove the engine cover.

3.  Remove in the order indicated in the table.

am3zzw00004118

1
Glow plug lead
2
Glow plug
(See Glow Plug Removal Note.)
(See Glow Plug Installation Note.)

4.  Install in the reverse order of removal.


Glow Plug Removal Note

1.  When removing the glow plug, first loosen it at least one pitch using a tool, then loosen by hand.


Glow Plug Installation Note

1.  Tighten the glow plug at least one pitch by hand, and continue tightening with a tool.
