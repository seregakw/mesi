DTC P1402

EGR metering orifice restricted
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the input voltage from the EGR valve position sensor signal. If the voltages at PCM terminal 129 deviates from the threshold, the PCM determines that the there is a EGR system range/performance malfunction.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  EGR valve malfunction
•  Air leakage in intake-air system
•  MAP sensor characteristic malfunction
•  EGR valve or pipe restriction or clogging
•  PCM malfunction
