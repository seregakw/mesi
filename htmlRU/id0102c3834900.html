DTC P115A

Low fuel level - forced limited power
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM compares the calculated engine torque based on the engine rotation fluctuation from the engine operating condition and on the CKP signal fluctuation. If these calculated engine torque difference is above specified when the fuel level signal from the instrument cluster is low, the PCM determines that reducing engine power is occurring from the fuel level low.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  No fuel in tank
•  Fuel sender gauge unit malfunction
•  Instrument cluster malfunction
•  PCM malfunction
