GLOW PLUG INSPECTION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)]

id0113b4804300

Примечание
•  Perform the following test only when directed.

1.  Remove the glow plug lead. (See GLOW PLUG REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

2.  Inspect for continuity between the glow plug terminal and the cylinder head using a tester.

•  If there is no continuity, replace the glow plug.
Resistance (Reference)
Approx. 1.3 ohms [20°C {68 °F}]
