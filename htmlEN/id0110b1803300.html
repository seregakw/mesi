VALVE CLEARANCE ADJUSTMENT [ZJ, ZY, Z6]

id0110b1803300

1.  Remove the battery cover.

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove the front wheel and tire (RH). (See GENERAL PROCEDURES (SUSPENSION).)

4.  Remove the air cleaner component. (Except U.K. specs.) (See INTAKE-AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

5.  Remove the air cleaner case and position the air cleaner cover out of the way with the PCM connector still connected. (U.K. specs.) (See INTAKE-AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

6.  Remove the ignition coils. (See IGNITION COIL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

7.  Disconnect the ventilation hose.

8.  Remove the cylinder head cover.

9.  Remove the drive belt. (See DRIVE BELT REPLACEMENT [ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

10.  Remove the engine front cover blind plug shown in the figure.

am3zzw00006290

11.  Insert a M6 bolt (45 to 80 mm {1.8—3.1 in} length bolt with threads to the end) into the service hole (right side) by hand as shown in the figure. Pull back the inserted bolt approx. 2 mm {0.08 in} from where it contacts the tensioner arm, and set the bolt in a position slightly outward of the tensioner arm.

am3zzw00000321

12.  Release tension on the timing chain.

(1)  Press down the link plate of the chain tensioner from the service hole on the left side using a thin flathead screwdriver (precision screwdriver) as shown in the figure. Then rotate the crank pulley backwards (counterclockwise) with the plunger lock released.
am3zzw00000322
Note
•  When the crankshaft pulley is turned counterclockwise, the timing chain loosens because the tensioner arm is pressed by the timing chain and the plunger of the chain tensioner is pressed in.
am3zzw00000323
(2)  Insert the M6 bolt installed to the engine front cover to the position where it catches the inside of the rib on the tensioner arm.
am3zzw00000325
Note
•  When the timing chain loosens, the tensioner arm moves to the position shown in the figure and presses the M6 bolt to secure the tensioner arm.
am3zzw00000324

13.  Fix the camshaft using a wrench on the cast hexagon.

am3zzw00000326

14.  Remove the camshaft sprocket installation bolt.

15.  Remove the timing sprocket on the exhaust side with the timing chain positioned out of the way.

am3zzw00000327

16.  Loosen the camshaft cap installation bolts in 2—3 passes in the order shown in the figure.

am3zzw00000328

17.  Remove the camshaft caps.

18.  Remove the intake and exhaust camshafts.

19.  Remove the tappets.

20.  Select a proper tappet according to the result of the valve clearance inspection and install it.

Tappet to be selected: thickness of the removed tappet + measured valve clearance – standard valve clearance (0.3 mm {0.0118 in})

Standard valve clearance [Engine cold]
0.27—0.33 mm {0.0107—0.0129 in}

21.  Align the timing marks on the crank pulley and the engine front cover, and then align the No.1 cylinder to the TDC.

am3zzw00000329

22.  Install the intake and exhaust camshafts with the No.1 cylinder so that it is near TDC of the compression stroke.

23.  Install the camshaft caps to the position shown in the figure, and temporarily tighten the No.2 and No.7 camshaft installation bolts.

am3zzw00000330

24.  Tighten the camshaft installation bolts in 2—3 passes uniformly in the order shown in the figure.

am3zzw00000331

Tightening torque
11.3—14.2 N·m {1.16—1.44 kgf·m, 8.4—10.4 ft·lbf}

25.  Align the sprocket timing marks on the intake and exhaust camshafts so that they form a straight line in alignment with the upper horizontal surface of the cylinder head.

am3zzw00000332

26.  Fix the camshaft using a wrench on the cast hexagon.

am3zzw00000326

27.  Tighten the camshaft sprocket installation bolt.

Tightening torque
49.6—60.8 N·m {5.06—6.19 kgf·m, 36.6—44.8 ft·lbf}

28.  Remove the M6 bolt holding the tensioner arm.

29.  Verify that there is no slack in the timing chain, and then verify that the marks on the camshaft sprocket and the crank pulley are aligned.

30.  Inspect the valve timing by rotating the crankshaft clockwise twice.

31.  Install the new engine front cover blind plug.

am3zzw00006290

Tightening torque
3.0—6.0 N·m {31—61 kgf·cm, 26.6—53.1 in·lbf}

32.  Install the drive belt. (See DRIVE BELT REPLACEMENT [ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

33.  Install the cylinder head cover. (See TIMING CHAIN REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

34.  Connect the ventilation hose.

35.  Install the ignition coils. (See IGNITION COIL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

36.  Install the air cleaner component. (See INTAKE-AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

37.  Install the front wheel and tire (RH). (See GENERAL PROCEDURES (SUSPENSION).)
