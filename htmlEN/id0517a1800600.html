AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE FLUID (ATF) REPLACEMENT[FN4A-EL]

id0517a1800600

Warning
•  A hot transaxle and ATF can cause severe burns. Turn off the engine and wait until they are cool before replacing the ATF.

1.  Remove the oil dipstick.

2.  Remove the under cover.

3.  Remove the oil drain plug and washer.

am3zzw00001199

4.  Drain the ATF into a container.

5.  Install a new washer and the drain plug.

Tightening torque
29.4—41.2 N·m
{3.0—4.2 kgf·m, 21.7—30.3 ft·lbf}

6.  Install the under cover.

7.  Add the specified ATF until ATF level reaches lower notch of dipstick type of ATF through the oil filler tube.

ATF type
ATF M‐V
ATF capacity (Approx. quantity)
ZY: 7.6 L {8.0 US qt, 6.7 lmp qt}
Z6: 7.0 L {7.4 US qt, 6.2 lmp qt}
LF: 7.2 L {7.6 US qt, 6.3 lmp qt}

8.  Ensure that the ATF level is in the HOT range (65 °C {149 °F}). (See AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE FLUID (ATF) INSPECTION[FN4A-EL])

•  Add ATF to the specified level as necessary.
