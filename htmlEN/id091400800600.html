1.  The following actuators and switches are integrated with the front door latch and lock actuator. Inspect the front door latch and lock actuator according to each inspection procedure for the following items.

•  Front door latch switch (See FRONT DOOR LATCH SWITCH INSPECTION.)
•  Front door key cylinder switch (driver’s side) (See FRONT DOOR KEY CYLINDER SWITCH INSPECTION.)
•  Door lock-link switch (driver’s side) (See DOOR LOCK-LINK SWITCH INSPECTION.)
