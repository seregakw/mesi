DTC B1317

Battery voltage high
DETECTION CONDITION
Input voltage from the battery is excessively high
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Battery malfunction
•  Generator malfunction
•  PJB malfunction

Diagnostic procedure

STEP

INSPECTION

ACTION
1
INSPECT DTC FROM PCM
•  Connect the M-MDS to the DLC-2.
•  Are any DTCs from the PCM displayed?
Yes
Perform DTC inspection.

(See DTC TABLE[LF ATX (FN4A-EL), L3 ATX].)

(See DTC TABLE[MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

No
Go to the next step.
2
VERIFY TROUBLESHOOTING COMPLETED

•  Clear the DTC from the PJB memory using the M-MDS.
•  Turn the ignition switch to LOCK position then ON position.
•  Is the same DTC present?
Yes
