FUEL FILTER COMPONENT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)]

id0114f1777800

1.  Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)].)

2.  Remove the battery and tray. (R.H.D.) (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)].)

3.  Complete the “BEFORE REPAIR PROCEDURE”. (See BEFORE REPAIR PROCEDURE [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)].)

4.  Remove in the order indicated in the table.

L.H.D.

am3zzw00004159

R.H.D.

am3zzw00004160

1
Connector
2
Fuel hose
3
Installation nut
4
Fuel filter body

5.  Install in the reverse order of removal.

6.  Complete the “AFTER REPAIR PROCEDURE”. (See AFTER REPAIR PROCEDURE [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)].)

7.  Start the engine and verify that fuel does not leak from the fuel system.

•  If fuel leaks, reinstall the fuel filter.
