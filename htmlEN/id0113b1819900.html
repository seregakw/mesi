Without Throttle Valve Actuator Only

1.  Remove the under cover.

2.  Remove the battery cover. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

3.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

4.  Remove the IAC valve.
