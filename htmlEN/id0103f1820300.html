id0103f1820300

5

WILL NOT CRANK
DESCRIPTION
•  Starter does not work.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Open starter circuit between the engine switch and starter
•  Starter malfunction
•  Seized/hydrolocked engine, flywheel
•  Immobilizer system (PATS) and/or circuit malfunction (if equipped)
•  Immobilizer system operating properly (engine key is not registered)
•  Advanced keyless entry system malfunction (if equipped)
•  Low or dead battery
•  Charging system malfunction
