TRANSAXLE FLUID TEMPERATURE (TFT) SENSOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[FN4A-EL]

id0517a1801100

Warning
•  A hot transaxle and ATF can cause severe burns. Turn off the engine and wait until they are cool before replacing the ATF.

1.  Remove the oil pan. (See CONTROL VALVE BODY REMOVAL[FN4A-EL].)

2.  Remove the control valve body. (See CONTROL VALVE BODY REMOVAL[FN4A-EL].)

3.  Remove the coupler component.

am3zzw00001226

4.  Install a coupler component.

5.  Install the control valve body. (See CONTROL VALVE BODY INSTALLATION[FN4A-EL].)

6.  Install the oil pan. (See CONTROL VALVE BODY INSTALLATION[FN4A-EL].)

7.  Perform the mechanical system test. (See MECHANICAL SYSTEM TEST[FN4A-EL].)
