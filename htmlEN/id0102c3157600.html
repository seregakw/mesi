DTC P0313

Misfire detected with low fuel
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM detects misfire when the fuel tank level is low and does not detect it when the fuel tank level is high. The PCM determines that the misfire is generated by the fuel tank level being too low.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Low fuel level
•  Fuel pump malfunction
•  Fuel injector malfunction
•  CMP sensor malfunction
•  CKP sensor malfunction
•  Inadequate engine compression due to engine internal malfunction
•  Related connector or terminal malfunction
•  Related wiring harness malfunction
•  PCM malfunction
