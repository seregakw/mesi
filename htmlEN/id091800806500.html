PANEL LIGHT CONTROL SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id091800806500

1.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2.  Detach the bonnet release lever from the lower panel. (See BONNET LATCH AND RELEASE LEVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[L.H.D.].) (See BONNET LATCH AND RELEASE LEVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[R.H.D.].)

3.  Remove the front scuff plate. (Driver’s side) (See FRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4.  Remove the front side trim. (Driver’s side) (See FRONT SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

5.  Remove the lower panel. (See LOWER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

6.  Remove the panel light control switch.

am3zzw00003667

7.  Disconnect the panel light control switch connector.

8.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
