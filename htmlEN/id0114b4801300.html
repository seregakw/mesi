Resistance Inspection

1.  Remove the battery cover. (SeeBATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[L3 Turbo].)

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove the fuel pump resister. (See FUEL PUMP RESISTOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[L3 Turbo].)

4.  Verify that the continuity between the fuel pump resister terminal A and B is within the specification.

•  If not within the specification, replace the fuel resistor.
Fuel pump resistor continuity
0.304—0.336 ohms (20 °C {68 °F})
