Warning
•  When the ignition switch is turned to the ON position, and the wiper and washer switch is in the AUTO position, the windshield wipers may operate automatically in the following cases. Always turn the ignition switch, and the wiper and washer switch off before handling the windshield wipers; otherwise a pinched hand or fingers could result in injury or a wiper system malfunction:
―  The upper part of the windshield rain sensor is touched.
―  The windshield receives an impact.
―  The rain sensor receives an impact from the cabin.
