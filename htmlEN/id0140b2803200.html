ACCELERATOR PEDAL POSITION (APP) SENSOR INSPECTION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)]

id0140b2803200

1.  Connect the M-MDS to the DLC-2.

am3zzw00001043

2.  Switch the ignition to ON.

3.  Verify that the APP sensor output signal (PID: APP) increases according to the increase in the accelerator opening angle when the accelerator opening angle is gradually increased.

•  If verified, go to the next step.
•  If not as verified, replace the accelerator pedal. (See ACCELERATOR PEDAL (APP) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

4.  Verify that the APP sensor output signal (PID: APP) is within the specification when the accelerator pedal is depressed and not depressed.

•  If not as specified, replace the accelerator pedal. (See ACCELERATOR PEDAL (APP) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)
