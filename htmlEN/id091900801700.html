WASHER TANK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id091900801700

1.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2.  Remove the front bumper. (See FRONT BUMPER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3.  Disconnect the windshield washer motor connector and windshield washer hose.

am3zzw00002099

4.  Disconnect the headlight cleaner motor connector and headlight cleaner hose. (Vehicles with the headlight cleaner)

5.  Disconnect the rear washer motor connector and rear washer hose. (5HB)

6.  Disconnect the washer fluid-level sensor connector. (Vehicles with the headlight cleaner)

7.  Remove the bolts and nut, then remove the washer tank.

8.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
