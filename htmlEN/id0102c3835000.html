DTC P115B

Low fuel level - forced engine shutdown
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM compares the calculated engine torque based on the engine rotation fluctuation from the engine operating condition and on the CKP signal fluctuation. If these calculated engine torque difference is excessive when the fuel level signal from the instrument cluster is low, the PCM determines that the fuel level is low to the engine stall level.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  No fuel in tank
•  Fuel sender gauge unit malfunction
•  Instrument cluster malfunction
•  PCM malfunction
