FRONT BELTLINE MOLDING REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id091600804800

1.  Remove the inner garnish. (See INNER GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2.  Remove the power outer mirror installation screws and move the mirror to access the front end of the front beltline molding. (See POWER OUTER MIRROR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3.  Raise the center of the front beltline molding and detach tabs A.

am3zzw00002206

4.   Using a small flathead screwdriver, detach clip B and remove the front beltline molding.

5.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
