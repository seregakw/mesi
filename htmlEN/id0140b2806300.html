INTAKE AIR TEMPERATURE (IAT) SENSOR NO.2 REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)]

id0140b2806300

1.  Remove the battery cover. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove the engine cover.

4.  Disconnect the IAT sensor No.2 connector.

5.  Remove the IAT sensor No.2 installation screw.

6.  Remove IAT sensor No.2 from the air pipe.
