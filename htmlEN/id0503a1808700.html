Engine stalls when shifted to D, M ranges, or in R position
DESCRIPTION
•  Engine stalls when shifting from N or P position to D, M ranges or R position at idle.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  The malfunction is on the engine control side (i.e. IAC system/Electronic throttle control system). Otherwise, the malfunction is in the input/turbine speed sensor (engine sometimes starts) or the TCC circuit (engine always stalls).
