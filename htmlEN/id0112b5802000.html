COOLING FAN MOTOR COMPONENT INSPECTION[LF, L3]

id0112b5802000

1.  Perform the cooling fan control system inspection. (See ENGINE CONTROL SYSTEM OPERATION INSPECTION[LF, L3].)

•  If the cooling fan control does not operate properly, inspect the following.
―  ECT sensor
―  A/C switch
―  A/C refrigerant pressure switch (High/low pressure)
―  A/C refrigerant pressure switch (Middle pressure)
―  VSS
―  A/C magnetic clutch
―  Fan control module power supply circuit
―  Fan control module ground circuit
―  Fan control signal circuit (between fan control module terminal B and PCM terminal 1W*1, 1R*2)
•  If the all items are normal, replace the cooling fan component.
*1  :LF ATX (FN4A-EL), L3 ATX
*2  :MTX, LF ATX (FS5A-EL)
