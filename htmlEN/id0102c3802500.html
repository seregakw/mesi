DTC P0089

Fuel Pressure Regulator Performance
DETECTION CONDITION
•  PCM detects the fuel pressure at the common rail for the following while the engine is running, the PCM determines that the fuel system pressure is not controlled properly.
―  Fuel pressure is excessively high.
―  Fuel pressure is higher than the threshold compared to the target fuel pressure.
―  Fuel pressure is lower than the threshold compared to the target fuel pressure.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Erratic signal the from fuel pressure sensor to PCM
•  Fuel metering control valve or related circuit malfunction
•  Fuel leakage from the fuel injector
•  Fuel leakage or clogging in fuel system
•  Supply pump malfunction
•  Fuel pressure limiter malfunction
•  Improper operation of fuel injection control
•  PCM malfunction
