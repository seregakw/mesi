LIFTGATE LATCH AND LOCK ACTUATOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id091400803800

1.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2.  Remove the liftgate upper trim. (See LIFTGATE UPPER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3.  Remove the liftgate lower trim. (See LIFTGATE LOWER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4.  Disconnect the liftgate lock actuator connector and liftgate latch switch connector.

5.  Detach the rod from the liftgate key cylinder.

6.  Remove the clips.

7.  Remove the bolts.

am3zzw00003638

8.  Remove the liftgate latch and lock actuator

9.  Detach the cable from the liftgate latch and lock actuator.

am3zzw00003639

10.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
