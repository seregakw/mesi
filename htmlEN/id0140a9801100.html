BAROMETRIC PRESSURE (BARO) SENSOR INSPECTION[LF, L3]

Note
•  Before performing the following inspection, make sure to follow the procedure as indicated in the troubleshooting flowchart. (SeeHOW TO USE THIS MANUAL.)

1.  Connect the M-MDS to the DLC‐2.

am3zzw00000466

2.  Turn the ignition switch to the ON position.

3.  Select BARO on the M-MDS.

4.  Verify that the BARO PID (pressure) and barometric pressure are practically equal.

•  If not as verified, replace the PCM. (See PCM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[LF, L3].)
