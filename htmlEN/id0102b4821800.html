DTC P0662

Variable intake air solenoid valve circuit high input
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the variable intake air solenoid valve control signal. If the PCM turns variable intake air solenoid valve on but voltage at PCM terminal still remains high, the PCM determines that the variable intake air solenoid valve circuit has malfunction.
Diagnostic support note
•  This is a continuous monitor (Other).
•  The MIL does not illuminate.
•  FREEZE FRAME DATA is not available.
•  The DTC is stored in the PCM memory.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Variable intake air solenoid valve malfunction
•  Short to power supply in wiring harness between variable intake air solenoid valve terminal A and PCM terminal 2J
•  Shorted variable intake air solenoid valve or PCM connector
•  PCM malfunction
