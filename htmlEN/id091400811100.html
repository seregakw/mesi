TRUNK LID LATCH AND OPENER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id091400811100

1.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2.  Remove the trunk lid trim. (See TRUNK LID TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3.  Detach the rod from the trunk lid key cylinder.

4.  Disconnect the trunk lid opener connector and the trunk lid latch switch connector.

am3zzw00003651

5.  Remove the bolts, then remove the trunk lid latch and opener.

6.  Install in the reverse order of removal.

7.  Adjust the trunk lid. (See TRUNK LID ADJUSTMENT.)
