WILL NOT CRANK
DESCRIPTION
•  The starter does not work.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Open circuit between the engine switch and the starter
•  Starter malfunction
•  Seized/hydrolocked engine, flywheel
•  Immobilizer system and/or circuit malfunction (Vehicles with immobilizer system)
•  Immobilizer system is operating properly (Engine key is not programmed.) (Vehicles with immobilizer system)
•  Low or dead battery
•  Charging system malfunction
