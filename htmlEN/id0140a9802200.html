Resistance Inspection

1.  Disconnect the mass air flow sensor connector.

2.  Verify that the resistance between MAF sensor terminals D and E is within the specification.

am3zzw00001748

•  If the monitor item status/specification (reference) is not within the specification, even though the IAT sensor resistance is within the specification, perform the “Circuit Open/Short Inspection”.
•  If not within the specification, replace the MAF sensor.

Circuit Open/Short Inspection

am3zzw00002665
acxuuw00000119

1.  Disconnect the PCM connector. (See PCM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[LF, L3].)

2.  Inspect the following wiring harnesses for open or short. (Continuity check)


Open circuit

•  If there is no continuity, the circuit is open. Repair or replace the harness.
―  MAF/IAT sensor terminal D and PCM terminal 1AT*1, 1AH*2
―  MAF/IAT sensor terminal E and PCM terminal 1AV*1, 1AA*2


,Short circuit

•  If there is continuity, the circuit is shorted. Repair or replace the harness.
―  MAF/IAT sensor terminal E and power supply
―  MAF/IAT sensor terminal D and power supply
―  MAF/IAT sensor terminal D and body ground

*1  :MTX, LF ATX (FS5A-EL)
*2  :LF ATX(FN4A-EL), L3 ATX
