DTC P2507

PCM B+ voltage low
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the voltage of back-up battery positive terminal at PCM terminal 1BA. If the PCM detected battery positive terminal voltage below 2.5 V for 2 s, the PCM determines that the backup voltage circuit has malfunction.
Diagnostic support note
•  This is a continuous monitor (CCM).
•  The MIL illuminates if PCM detects the above malfunction condition during the first drive cycle.
•  FREEZE FRAME DATA is available.
•  DTC is stored in PCM memory.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Meltdown fuse
•  Open circuit in wiring between fuse and PCM terminal 1BA
•  Short to ground between fuse and PCM terminal 1BA
•  Poor connection of PCM connector
•  PCM malfunction
