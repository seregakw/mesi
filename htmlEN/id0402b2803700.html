DTC B1342, C1267, C1730 [DYNAMIC STABILITY CONTROL (DSC)]

DTC
B1342, C1267
C1730
DSC CM (internal malfunction)
Brake fluid pressure sensor power supply system
DETECTION CONDITION
•  B1342, C1267
―  The DSC CM on-board diagnostic function detects control module internal malfunction.
•  C1730
―  Abnormal voltage to the brake fluid pressure sensor is detected.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  DSC CM internal malfunction

Diagnostic procedure

STEP

INSPECTION

ACTION
1
VERIFY NO DSC CM MALFUNCTION
•  Clear the DTCs from the memory.
(See ON-BOARD DIAGNOSIS [DYNAMIC STABILITY CONTROL (DSC)].)
•  Start the engine and drive the vehicle at 10 km/h {6.2 mph} or more.
•  Are the same DTCs present?
Yes
Replace the DSC CM, then go to the next step.
(See DSC HU/CM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
No
Go to the next step.
2
VERIFY THAT NO OTHER DTCS ARE PRESENT
•  Are any other DTCs output?
Yes
Go to the applicable DTC inspection.
(See ON-BOARD DIAGNOSIS [DYNAMIC STABILITY CONTROL (DSC)].)
No
DTC troubleshooting completed.
