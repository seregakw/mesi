COLUMN COVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id091700800800

1.  Remove the front scuff plate. (Driver’s side) (See FRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2.  Remove the front side trim. (Driver’s side) (See FRONT SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3.  Remove the lower panel. (See LOWER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4.  Detach the fit of the upper column cover from the meter hood rubber.

am3zzw00002198

5.  Remove the upper column cover.

6.  Remove the ignition key illumination.

7.  Remove the screws.

8.  Remove the lower column cover.

9.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
