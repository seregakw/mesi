ENGINE OIL LEVEL INSPECTION[L3 Turbo]

id0111b4800500

1.  Position the vehicle on level ground.

2.  Warm up the engine.

3.  Stop the engine and allow approx. 5 min before continuing.

4.  Remove the dipstick and verify that the oil level is between the MIN and MAX marks on the dipstick.

•  If the oil level is below the MIN mark, add engine oil.
