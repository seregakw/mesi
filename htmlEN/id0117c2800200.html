Warning
•  When the battery cables are connected, touching the vehicle body with generator terminal B will generate sparks. This can cause personal injury, fire, and damage to the electrical components. Always disconnect the battery negative cables before performing the following operation.

1.  Remove the battery cover. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[LF, L3].)

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove the under cover and splash shield as a single unit.

4.  Remove the plug hole plate. (See PLUG HOLE PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[LF, L3].)

5.  Set the drive belt out of the way. (See DRIVE BELT REPLACEMENT[LF, L3].)

6.  Set the coolant reserve tank out of the way.

7.  Remove in the order indicated in the table.

8.  Install in the reverse order of removal.

Generator Installation Note

1.  Match the generator fixing hole and engine side hole, then temporarily tighten the generator installation bolts in the order A, B, C, and D.

2.  Securely tighten the generator installation bolts in the order A, B, C, and D.
