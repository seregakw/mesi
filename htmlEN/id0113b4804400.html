GLOW PLUG REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)]

id0113b4804400

1.  Remove the battery cover. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove the engine cover.

4.  Remove in the order indicated in the table.

am3zzw00001452

1
Glow plug lead stay
2
Glow plug nuts
3
Glow plug lead
4
Glow plug

5.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
