PASSENGER JUNCTION BOX (PJB) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id094000800700

Caution
•  When replacing the PJB, the configuration procedure must be performed before removing the PJB. Replacing the PJB without performing the configuration procedure will result in system malfunction.

1.  Perform the PJB configuration. (See PASSENGER JUNCTION BOX (PJB) CONFIGURATION.)

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove in the order indicated in the table.
