DTC P2141

Intake throttle valve actuator circuit low
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors that the intake throttle valve actuator control circuit voltage at the PCM terminal 247. If the PCM terminal 247 voltage is below threshold when the intake throttle operation condition. The PCM determines that intake throttle valve actuator circuit is low.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Intake throttle malfunction
•  Connector or terminal malfunction
•  Short to ground circuit in wiring between intake throttle valve actuator terminal C and PCM terminal 247
•  Open circuit in wiring between intake throttle valve actuator terminal C and PCM terminal 247
•  Open circuit in wiring between intake throttle valve actuator terminal D and PCM control relay terminal E
•  PCM malfunction
