TRUNK LID ADJUSTMENT

id091000801700

1.  Measure the gap and height difference between the trunk lid and the body.

2.  Loosen the trunk lid hinge installation bolts and adjust the trunk lid.

•  If necessary, loosen the trunk lid lock striker installation bolts and adjust the trunk lid. (SeeTRUNK LID LOCK STRIKER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

Standard clearance

3.  Tighten the bolts.
