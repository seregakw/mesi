DRIVE BELT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)]

id0110f1801600

1.  Remove the under cover and splash shield (RH).

2.  Turn the center of the tensioner pulley clockwise to release tension to the drive belt.

am3zzw00003939

3.  Remove the drive belt.

4.  Reinstall the drive belt or install a new drive belt.

5.  Verify that the drive belt auto tensioner indicator mark does not exceed the limit. (See DRIVE BELT INSPECTION [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)].)

•  If it exceeds the limit, replace the drive belt.

6.  Install the under cover.

7.  Install the splash shield (RH).
