Air Intake Wire

1.  Set the REC/FRESH lever at recirculate position.

am3zzw00003269

2.  Connect the air intake wire to air intake link.

3.  Clamp the air intake wire to wire clamp.

4.  Remove the screwdriver and verify that the REC/FRESH lever can move its full stroke.


Air Mix Wire

1.  Set the temperature control dial at middle position (MAX HOT—MAX COLD).

2.  Connect the air mix wire to air mix link.

3.  Clamp the air mix wire to wire clamp.

4.  Remove the screwdriver and verify that the temperature control dial can move its full stroke.
