WATER PUMP REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[ZJ, ZY, Z6]

id0112c1800700

Warning
•  Remove and install all parts when the engine is cold, otherwise they can cause severe burns or serious injury.

1.  Remove the battery cover. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove the under cover and splash shield as a single unit.

4.  Drain the engine coolant. (See ENGINE COOLANT REPLACEMENT[ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

5.  Set the coolant reserve tank out of the way.

6.  Remove the drive belt. (See DRIVE BELT REPLACEMENT[ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

7.  Set the power steering fluid tank out of the way.

8.  Remove the generator. (See GENERATOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

9.  Remove in the order indicated in the table.

am3zzw00002946

1
Water pump component
2
O-ring

10.  Install in the reverse order of removal.

11.  Add engine coolant. (See ENGINE COOLANT REPLACEMENT[ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

12.  Inspect for engine coolant leakage. (See ENGINE COOLANT LEAKAGE INSPECTION[ZJ, ZY, Z6].)
