TRANSAXLE OIL REPLACEMENT [G35M-R]

id0515b1800100

Caution
•  There are two types of transaxle oil for this type of transaxle corresponding to the vehicle identification number. If the engine oil is replaced or supplemented, add oil which corresponds to the vehicle identification number and do not use oil of a different type. If a different type of oil is added, it could result in a transaxle malfunction.

1.  Park the vehicle on level ground.

2.  Remove the under cover.

3.  Remove the drain plug with the washer.

4.  Drain the oil into a suitable container.

5.  Install a new washer and the drain plug.
