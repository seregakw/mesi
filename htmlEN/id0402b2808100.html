DTC U1900, U2012, U2023, U2202 [DYNAMIC STABILITY CONTROL (DSC)]

id0402b2808100

DTC
U1900, U2012, U2023, U2202
CAN line
DETECTION CONDITION
•  U1900, U2023, U2202
―  Communication error with PCM is detected in CAN communication.
•  U2012
―  CAN system wiring harness open or short circuit is detected.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Incorrect vehicle data (suspension and brake specs.) input during PCM configuration
•  PCM configuration error
•  CAN system wiring harness open or short circuit with PCM
•  PCM malfunction

Diagnostic procedure
•  Inspect according to the diagnostic procedure in BODY & ACCESSORIES. (See DTC TABLE [MULTIPLEX COMMUNICATION SYSTEM].)
