BRAKE SWITCH INSPECTION[MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)]

id0411008019a1

Caution
•  Inspect the brake switch with it installed to the brake pedal, otherwise the brake switch may not operate normally. If the brake switch is removed from the brake pedal, replace the brake switch with a new one.

1.  Detach the bonnet release lever from the lower panel. (See BONNET LATCH AND RELEASE LEVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[L.H.D.].) (See BONNET LATCH AND RELEASE LEVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[R.H.D.].)

2.  Remove the front scuff plate. (Driver-side) (See FRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3.  Remove the front side trim. (Driver-side) (See FRONT SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4.  Remove the lower panel. (See LOWER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

5.  Remove the column cover. (See COLUMN COVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

6.  Disconnect the brake switch connector.

7.  Verify that the continuity is as indicated in the table.

•  If not as indicated in the table, replace the brake switch. (See BRAKE PEDAL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)
