VARIABLE SWIRL SHUTTER VALVE ACTUATOR INSPECTION[L3 Turbo]

id0113b5801200

1.  Remove the air hose. (See INTAKE AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[L3 Turbo].)

2.  Disconnect the vacuum hose from the variable swirl control solenoid valve.

3.  Connect a vacuum pump to the variable swirl shutter valve actuator.

4.  Apply vacuum and verify that the rod moves.

•  If the rod does not move, replace the intake manifold. (See INTAKE AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[L3 Turbo].)
