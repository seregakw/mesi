EGR VALVE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [LF, L3]

id0116c2801000

1.  Remove the plug hole plate. (See PLUG HOLE PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [LF, L3].)

2.  Remove the battery cover. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [LF, L3].)

3.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

4.  Disconnect the EGR valve connector.

5.  Remove in the order indicated in the table.

6.  Install in the reverse order of removal.

am3zzw00000995

1
Water hose
(See Water Hose Removal Note.)
2
EGR valve
(See EGR Valve Removal Note.)


Water Hose Removal Note

1.  Drain the engine coolant from the radiator. (See ENGINE COOLANT REPLACEMENT [LF, L3].)


EGR Valve Removal Note

1.  Remove the air hose. (See INTAKE-AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [LF, L3].)

2.  Remove the radiator hose (upper, engine side). (See RADIATOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [LF, L3].)
