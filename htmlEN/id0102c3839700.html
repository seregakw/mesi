DTC P1931

Fuel additive pump control circuit high
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The fuel additive control module monitors the fuel additive pump control circuit at the fuel additive control module terminal 9. If the fuel additive control module terminal 9 voltage above specified when the fuel additive pump turned to off, the fuel additive control module determines that the fuel additive pump control circuit high.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Short to power between the fuel additive control module terminal 9 and fuel additive pump terminal D
•  Short to power between the fuel additive control module terminal 10 and fuel additive pump terminal C
•  Fuel additive pump malfunction
