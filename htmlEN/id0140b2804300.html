MANIFOLD ABSOLUTE PRESSURE (MAP) SENSOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)]

id0140b2804300

1.  Remove the battery cover. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove the engine cover.

4.  Disconnect the MAP sensor connector.

5.  Remove the MAP sensor installation screw.

6.  Remove the MAP sensor from the air pipe.
