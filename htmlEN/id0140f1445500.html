ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE (ECT) SENSOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)]

id0140f1445500

Warning
•  When the engine is hot, it can badly burn. Turn off the engine and wait until it is cool before removing the ECT sensor.

1.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2.  Drain the engine coolant. (See ENGINE COOLANT REPLACEMENT [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)].)

3.  Remove the common rail. (See SUPPLY PUMP REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MZR-CD (RF Turbo)].)

4.  Remove in the order indicated in the table.

am3zzw00004206

1
ECT sensor
2
Gasket

5.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
