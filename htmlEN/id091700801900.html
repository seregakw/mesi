A-PILLAR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id091700801900

1.  Partially peel back the seaming welt.

2.  Detach clips A using a fastener remover.

am3zzw00002183

3.  Pull the A-pillar trim and detach clip B (1).

4.  Pull the A-pillar trim upward and remove clip B from the A-pillar trim (2).

5.  Pull clip B out and rotate it 45°.

am3zzw00002184

6.  Remove clip B from the grommet by pulling it upward.

7.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
