DTC P0140

A/F sensor no activity
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The PCM monitors the input signal from A/F sensor while the engine running. The A/F sensor signal does not change to rich from lean or to lean from rich for 100 s after the engine started. The PCM determines that the A/F sensor is not activity.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  A/F sensor deterioration
•  A/F sensor heater malfunction
•  Leakage exhaust system
•  Open circuit or short to ground in wiring harness between A/F sensor terminal A and PCM terminal 93
•  Open circuit or short to ground in wiring harness between A/F sensor terminal B and PCM terminal 85
•  Insufficient compression
•  Engine malfunction
