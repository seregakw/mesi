OIL CONTROL VALVE (OCV) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[LF, L3]

id0110a0801300

1.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2.  Remove the following parts.

•  Plug hole plate (See PLUG HOLE PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[LF, L3].)
•  Ignition coils (See IGNITION COIL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[LF, L3].)
•  Oil control valve (OCV) connector
•  Ventilation hose
•  Cylinder head cover
•  Oil control valve (OCV)

3.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
