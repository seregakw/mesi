CURRENT SENSOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[ZJ, ZY, Z6]

id0140b1805400


With Throttle Valve Actuator Only

Warning
•  If the negative battery cable contacts a metal part, it can cause fire or damage to the electrical components.When removing the current sensor, be careful not to contact a metal part, and always hold the negative battery cable in your hand.

1.  Remove the battery cover. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove in the order indicated in the table.

am3zzw00003399

1
Connector
2
Nut
3
Current sensor

4.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
