DTC C1099 [ELECTRO HYDRAULIC POWER ASSIST STEERING (EHPAS)]

id0602b1800600

DTC
C1099
Electric power steering oil pump (motor)
DETECTION CONDITION
•  The EHPAS control module detects that the motor speed is less than the specified value.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  Power steering oil pump internal malfunction
•  Motor internal malfunction

Diagnostic procedure

STEP

INSPECTION

ACTION
1
REPLACE POWER STEERING OIL PUMP
•  Replace the power steering oil pump, then go to the next step.
(See ELECTRIC POWER STEERING OIL PUMP COMPONENT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [ZY, LF, L3, MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)
(See ELECTRIC POWER STEERING OIL PUMP COMPONENT DISASSEMBLY/ASSEMBLY [ZY, LF, L3, MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)
2
VERIFY THAT HE SAME DTC IS PRESENT
•  Clear the DTC from the memory.
(See ON-BOARD DIAGNOSIS [ELECTRO HYDRAULIC POWER ASSIST STEERING (EHPAS)].)
•  Is the same DTC present?
Yes
Replace the electric power steering oil pump component.
(See ELECTRIC POWER STEERING OIL PUMP COMPONENT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [ZY, LF, L3, MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)
No
Go to the next step.
3
VERIFY THAT NO OTHER DTCS ARE PRESENT
•  Are any other DTCs output?
Yes
Go to the applicable DTC inspection.
(See ON-BOARD DIAGNOSIS [ELECTRO HYDRAULIC POWER ASSIST STEERING (EHPAS)].)
No
DTC troubleshooting completed.
