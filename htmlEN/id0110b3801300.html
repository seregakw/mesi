OIL CONTROL VALVE (OCV) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[L3 Turbo]

1.  Remove the battery cover.

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove the charge air cooler. (See INTAKE AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[L3 Turbo].)

4.  Remove the ignition coils. (See IGNITION COIL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[L3 Turbo].)

5.  Disconnect the wiring harness.

6.  Remove the ventilation hose.

7.  Remove the cylinder head cover. (See TIMING CHAIN REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[L3 Turbo].)

8.  Remove the OCV.
