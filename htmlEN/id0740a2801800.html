1.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2.  Remove the following parts:

(1)  Ashtray panel
(2)  Detach the bonnet release lever from the lower panel. (See BONNET LATCH AND RELEASE LEVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[L.H.D.].) (See BONNET LATCH AND RELEASE LEVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[R.H.D.].)
(3)  Front scuff plate (See FRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4)  Front side trim (See FRONT SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(5)  Lower panel (See LOWER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(6)  Decoration panel (See DECORATION PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(7)  Glove compartment (See GLOVE COMPARTMENT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(8)  PJB and PJB bracket (See PASSENGER JUNCTION BOX (PJB) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(9)  Console (See CONSOLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(10)  Driver-side air bag module (R.H.D., MTX) (See DRIVER-SIDE AIR BAG MODULE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(11)  Column cover (R.H.D., MTX) (See COLUMN COVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(12)  Meter hood (R.H.D., MTX) (See METER HOOD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(13)  Instrument cluster (R.H.D., MTX) (See INSTRUMENT CLUSTER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(14)  Steering shaft (R.H.D., MTX) (See STEERING WHEEL AND COLUMN REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3.  Detach the PJB wiring harness clamp from the dashboard.

4.  Remove the center panel unit. (See CENTER PANEL UNIT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

5.  Remove the air intake wire (Australian, General (L.H.D. R.H.D.) specs.), air mix wire and airflow mode wire with wire clamp from the A/C unit.

MTX vehicles
1.  Remove the wire clamp screws from the A/C unit using a ratchet offset screwdriver (commercially available tool) and disconnect the air mix wire with the wire clamp still installed.

2.  Remove the wire clamp screws from the A/C unit and disconnect the airflow mode wire from the A/C unit with the wire clamp still installed.
ATX vehicles
1.  Remove the wire clamp screws from the A/C unit and disconnect the air mix, airflow mode wire from the A/C unit with the wire clamp still installed.
