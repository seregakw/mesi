FUEL-FILLER SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)]

id0140b2806000

1.  Remove the battery cover. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[MZ-CD 1.6 (Y6)].)

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Pinch the tabs and pull the fuel-filler switch outward.

4.  Disconnect the connector and remove the fuel-filler switch.

am3zzw00001596

5.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
