Coil resistance inspection

1.  Remove the battery cover.

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Disconnect the OCV connector.

4.  Measure the coil resistance between terminals A and B using a tester.

am3zzw00001871

•  If not within the specification, replace the OCV.
OCV resistance
6.9—7.9 ohms [20°C {68°F}]

5.  Connect the OCV connector.


Spool Valve Operation Inspection

1.  Remove the battery cover.

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove the OCV. (See OIL CONTROL VALVE (OCV) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[ZJ, ZY, Z6].)

4.  Verify that the spool valve inside the OCV is at the maximum valve timing retard position as shown in the figure.

am3zzw00001872

•  If it cannot be verified, replace the OCV.

5.  Verify that the battery is fully charged.

6.  Apply battery positive voltage between oil control valve terminals A and B, and verify that the spool valve operates and moves to the maximum valve timing advance position.

am3zzw00001871

•  If there is any malfunction, replace the OCV.
Note
•  When applying battery positive voltage between the oil control valve terminals, the connection of the negative and positive cable can be either the A or B terminal.

7.  Stop applying battery positive voltage and verify that the spool valve returns to the maximum valve timing retard position.

am3zzw00001873

•  If it cannot be verified, replace the OCV.

8.  Install the OCV. (See OIL CONTROL VALVE (OCV) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[ZJ, ZY, Z6].)
