CRUISE CONTROL SWITCH INSPECTION[LF, L3]

id0120b2800100

1.  Remove the battery cover (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[LF, L3].)

2.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3.  Remove the driver‐side air bag module. (See DRIVER-SIDE AIR BAG MODULE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4.  Disconnect the cruise control switch connector. (See AUDIO CONTROL SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

5.  Inspect for resistance and continuity between cruise control switch terminals A—G using a tester.

Circuit Open/Short Inspection

1.  Disconnect the PCM connector. (See PCM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[LF, L3].)

2.  Disconnect the cruise control switch connector. (See AUDIO CONTROL SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3.  Inspect the following wiring harnesses for an open or short circuit (continuity check).

am3zzw00002693
am3zzw00002694

Open circuit
•  If there is no continuity, the circuit is open. Repair or replace the wiring harness.
―  Cruise control switch terminal A and PCM terminal 1AQ*1, 1AD*2
―  Cruise control switch terminal G and body ground
Short circuit
•  If there is continuity, the circuit is short. Repair or replace the wiring harness.
―  Cruise control switch terminal A and body ground
―  Cruise control switch terminal G and power supply
*1  :MTX, LF ATX (FS5A-EL)
*2  :LF ATX (FN4A-EL), L3 ATX
