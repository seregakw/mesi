LIFTGATE SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id091700803700

1.  Remove the liftgate upper trim. (See LIFTGATE UPPER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2.  Pull the point A of the strap hanger as shown in the figure.

am3zzw00001942

3.  Remove the strap hanger.

4.  Pull the liftgate side trim outward and detach clip A, locator pin B.

am3zzw00001943

5.  Detach the tab C and remove the liftgate side trim.

6.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
