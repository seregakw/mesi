1.  Disconnect the PCM connector. (See PCM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION[LF, L3].)

2.  Inspect the following harness for open or short. (Continuity check)

Open circuit
•  Neutral switch terminal A and PCM terminal 1X
•  Neutral switch terminal B and body ground
Short circuit
•  Neutral switch terminal A and power supply
•  Neutral switch terminal B and body ground
