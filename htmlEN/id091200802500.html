POWER OUTER MIRROR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id091200802500

1.  Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2.  Remove the inner garnish. (See INNER GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3.  Disconnect the power outer mirror connector.

am3zzw00001921

4.  Remove the screws.

5.  Push the power outer mirror against the vehicle and detach the tab while lifting the mirror up to remove.

6.  Install in the reverse order of removal.
