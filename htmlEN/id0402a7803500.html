DTC
U1900, U2012
CAN line
DETECTION CONDITION
•  U1900
―  Communication error with PCM is detected in CAN communication.
•  U2012
―  CAN system wiring harness open or short circuit is detected.
POSSIBLE CAUSE
•  CAN system wiring harness open or short circuit with PCM
•  PCM malfunction


Diagnostic procedure

•  Inspect according to diagnostic procedure in BODY & ACCESSORIES. (See DTC TABLE[MULTIPLEX COMMUNICATION SYSTEM].)
